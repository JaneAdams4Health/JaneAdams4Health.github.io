About Me

Jane Adams Nutritional therapist Bsc Hons, Dip CNM, BANTm, CNHCreg

What motivates me?

From an early age I have always had an interest in food and helping people. This initially led me to study a degree in hotel and catering management which then led onto a career in Accountancy. However, after having my first two children I became increasingly aware of what I was feeding them and also what chemicals I was using around the house and the potential harm that a poor diet and toxic load could have on our health. Not wanting to go back to Accountancy work I initially began attending some talks by a local Nutritional Therapist who had studied with the College of Naturopathic Medicine (CNM). I was so interested in the information she was sharing regarding the powerful effect that food can have on our health, I decided that I would like to retrain as a Nutritional Therapist. However it was only afterI lost my Father to bowel cancer that I finally decided the time was right and that what I wanted to do was help people to improved health. So in 2012 I started studying with CNM. It was a tough 3 years but I loved every minute of it and I proudly received my diploma in October 2015.

I am also a Tropic Skincare Ambassador. Tropic is a British company based in Surrey that makes natural skincare products and make-up heavily based on botanicals.

I strongly believe that prevention is better than cure and that food and lifestyle choices can have a massive influence on your health whether that be negative or positive depending on the choices made.

I am excited at what the future may hold for you so let me support you with whatever health issues you may have. If you don’t have any and would like to stay healthy let me advise you on the best way you can do this.
